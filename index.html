Software Engineer

After three years as an accountant at a Deaf-owned business, I realized I wanted to make an impact a different way. After deciding to transition to software engineering, I chose Holberton School because of their proven track record of helping people change their careers and because I felt their project-based model of education was a perfect fit for me. I recently completed the year-long Foundations Curriculum at Holberton and am currently looking for opportunities to contribute my Python backend skills to a socially conscious company committed to doing good.

I am hoping to work with people who are as excited about food, reading, and bicycling as I am.
